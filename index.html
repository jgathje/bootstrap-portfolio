About Me

My name is Joe Gathje. I am new to coding. So far, I have found it both challenging and fun. My favorite thing about coding so far is writing javascript/jquery. I thoroughly enjoy the challenge of getting things to function and display properly.

My interest in coding began many years ago. When I was in high school, I wrote a "manifesto" by hand. On paper. My friends passed it around and and read it, but over time, it got fairly beat up. Instead of risking losing my hard work, I decided to see if I could build a website to display. I came across Angelfire and began to look up basic HTML and CSS styling elements. Before long, I had a built my own functioning page. Over the next few years, I built several rudimentary, but functioning websites. For some reason, it took until now for me to realize I could turn that hobby into a career.

Previously, I worked in fincance. I spent time at both Ameriprise and AllianceOne in phone roles. I spent nearly a decade before that working in the service industry, mostly in restaurant management. My time in restaurants has taught me to think quickly on my feet and come up with creative solutions to complex problems.
